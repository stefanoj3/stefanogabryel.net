About me

Who am I and what to expect when hiking with me

I’m a certified hiking guide (GAE) and nature photographer based in Tuscany. My passion is to show travelers the authentic side of this land — quiet forests, hidden mountain trails, ancient villages, and the pure beauty of wild Tuscany. My tours are designed for small private groups, offering a slow and personal way to discover the region — with the eye of a photographer and the knowledge of a local.

  • Locations

    The places I guide through are carefully selected for their biodiversity, natural beauty, and peaceful atmosphere. These are areas rarely visited by mass tourism — quiet forests, hidden ridges, and valleys where nature still feels untouched.

  • Group Size

    My hikes are always private, for up to 8 participants. This allows me to adapt each experience to your pace, interests, and the rhythm of the landscape.

  • Difficulty & Customization

    Routes range from easy walks to more demanding trails. Each hike can be tailored to your fitness level, desired duration, and season.

  • Equipment

    Comfortable hiking boots, layered clothing, and a small backpack are required.

  • Seasonality

    Tuscany’s nature changes beautifully throughout the year. From spring flowers to autumn forests and mushroom foraging, every season has its unique charm.

  • I’ll make sure you return home with lasting memories worth sharing

    Even if you’re not a photographer, I’ll help you discover the best light, angles, and viewpoints — so you can return home with more than just memories.
